This application has functionality where the user can log their last 2 prompts and the last 2 responses from the Phisherman into a MongoDB Atlas database.

This is done by clicking the 'Submit' button after interacting with the AI. The user is advised to not enter any personal or presonally identifiable information into the AI and press 'Submit' as this will store their logs in the database. The AI won't store information without this button being clicked.

If you accidentally submit personal or personally identifiable data, please get in touch with myself (the developer) at this email address: kianboylan115@gmail.com and it will be removed.
